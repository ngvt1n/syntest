About This Study

This platform is designed to identify and study synesthesia through a series of carefully designed assessments. Participants will complete screening questionnaires and specialized tests to determine if they experience synesthetic patterns.

All data collected is anonymized and used solely for research purposes, in compliance with ethical research standards.

Platform Features

Pre-Screening

Complete an initial questionnaire to determine eligibility and receive personalized test recommendations based on your experiences.

Specialized Tests

Take scientifically designed tests for Trigger-Color, Trigger-Gustatory, and Sequence-Space synesthesia types.

Personal Dashboard

Track your progress, view test results, and access your personalized synesthesia profile through your secure dashboard.

Types of Synesthesia Tests

Trigger-Color Synesthesia

Tests for associations between triggers (letters, numbers, sounds) and specific colors. Includes color consistency and speed congruency assessments.

Trigger-Gustatory Synesthesia

Evaluates experiences where certain triggers evoke specific taste sensations.

Sequence-Space Synesthesia

Assesses the visualization of sequences (numbers, days, months) in specific spatial arrangements.

Comprehensive Analysis

Results are analyzed for consistency and patterns to provide insights into your synesthetic experiences.

For Researchers

Access aggregated participant data, export datasets, and analyze results through the researcher dashboard. All data is anonymized and complies with research ethics standards.
