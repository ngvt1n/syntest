Synesthesia? Who is she?

Synesthesia is a rare condition affecting a small amount of the population. It has been shown to have connections to artistic characteristics and has been shown to occur more frequently in some psychological disorders such as autism (Neufeld et al., 2021). Synesthesia has been difficult to study as there has been no comprehensive empirically tested screening battery available to researchers to say point blank if a participant has synesthesia or not. ....
